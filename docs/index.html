Program Design
Conditioning-First
4×/week full-body circuits · 3-week progressive → 1-week deload
5th day: class (untracked bonus conditioning)
• Conditioning
Every session is the cardio. 30–60s rest, 40/20 circuits, heart rate elevated for the full 45 minutes. No dedicated cardio blocks — the work is the conditioning.
• Weight Loss
Four dense sessions + class = 5 days of elevated metabolic output. The deficit for −4 kg is driven by work density, not food restriction alone. One compound per session preserves muscle.
• Muscle
Maintained, not chased. Full-body frequency with moderate reps is enough to preserve lean mass during the cut. No hypertrophy blocks.
4-Week Cycle
Weeks 1–3: Progress by reducing rest, adding reps, or adding circuit rounds. Weights stay steady — conditioning adapts first. Week 4: Deload. Same movements, half volume, full rest. Protects against burnout across 5 training days while targeting −4 kg.
